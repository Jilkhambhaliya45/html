PAV BHAJI

RECIPE
Ingredients:

For the Bhaji:

3 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, cauliflower), boiled and mashed 1 large onion, finely chopped 1 large tomato, finely chopped 1/2 cup capsicum (bell pepper), finely chopped 1/4 cup green peas, boiled 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced 1-inch piece of ginger, minced 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped (adjust to taste) 1 teaspoon pav bhaji masala 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste) 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder Salt to taste Butter or oil for cooking

For the Pav:

8 pav buns Butter for toasting For Garnish: Chopped coriander leaves Finely chopped onions Lemon wedges

Instructions:

Heat butter or oil in a pan. Add minced ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Saut√© for a minute until fragrant. Add chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes. Add the boiled and mashed potatoes and mixed vegetables. Mix everything thoroughly. Add boiled green peas and chopped capsicum. Mix and cook for another 5-7 minutes, mashing the vegetables with a potato masher or the back of a spoon as you cook. Adjust seasoning according to your taste. Add more pav bhaji masala or chili powder if desired. For the pav, slit the buns horizontally without separating the halves. Heat some butter on a griddle and toast the buns until they are lightly crispy and golden. Serve the hot bhaji with the toasted pav buns, garnished with chopped coriander leaves, finely chopped onions, and lemon wedges. Enjoy your delicious homemade pav bhaji! Feel free to customize the recipe by adding other vegetables, adjusting spices, and using more or less butter as per your preference.
